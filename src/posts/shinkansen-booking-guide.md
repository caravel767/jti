## The Easiest Method: Booking via Klook
While official JR websites exist, they often have issues with foreign credit cards. Booking through an authorized third-party like **Klook** is the smoothest experience.
*   **Foreign Card Friendly:** Accepts all major international credit cards.
*   **QR Code Boarding:** Receive a QR code and skip the ticket machine.
