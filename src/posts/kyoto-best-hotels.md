Choosing the right accommodation in Kyoto can make or break your trip. To avoid the notorious midday crowds, the best strategy is to stay within walking distance of major attractions. Here are our top boutique hotel recommendations.

## 1. Celestine Kyoto Gion
Located in the heart of the historic Gion district, this hotel is a serene oasis. You can easily walk to Kiyomizu-dera Temple before the tour buses arrive.
*   **Best for:** Couples and culture lovers.

## 3. Park Hyatt Kyoto
Set in the heart of Higashiyama, this hotel is perhaps the most prestigious stay in the city. It blends modern luxury with the timeless beauty of the surrounding Kodai-ji Temple area.
*   **Best for:** Luxury travelers seeking the absolute best location and views.
