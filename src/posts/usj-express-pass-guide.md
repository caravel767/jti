Universal Studios Japan (USJ) is famously crowded. Going without a strategy can result in spending 70% of your day in queues.

## Why the Express Pass is Worth the Money
An Express Pass 4 or 7 often includes a **timed-entry ticket to Super Nintendo World**. Without this, you might not even get into the Mario area.
